Our process

From first conversation to final support.

  1. Discover

    We listen first: project scope, drawings, lifestyle, priorities, budget, and timing.

  2. Plan

    We shape appliance packages around the kitchen, not around a generic product list.

  3. Coordinate

    We help align specs, dimensions, panels, ventilation, installation needs, and trade questions.

  4. Deliver

    We keep the delivery stage organized so the right products arrive with fewer surprises.

  5. Support

    We stay connected after delivery for questions, clarifications, and long-term confidence.

About KWA

A family business built on relationships.

KWA is a family-owned appliance destination serving homeowners, custom home builders, interior designers and architects across Southern Ontario. The name represents family, and the philosophy is personal: give every client honest guidance, careful coordination, and someone who stands behind the project.

We do not believe there is one best appliance for everyone. Our role is to understand the kitchen, the household, and the project before recommending what belongs in the home.
